Design Process - Poster Illustration

Project Description

As you can see, for most of my designs I will do about 40 doodles, spending 2 minutes on each one. I will then spend about 7-10 minutes doing my main sketches for the final design. For the project you see to the left, I had to design a poster describing a room facilitating 'Early Childhood' without actually using any text. I decided to focus my idea on nursery rhymes.


Requirements

  • Sketching
  • Adobe Illustrator
  • Adobe Photoshop
